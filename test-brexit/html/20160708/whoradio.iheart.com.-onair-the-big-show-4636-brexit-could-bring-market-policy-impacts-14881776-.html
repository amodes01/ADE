1040 WHO is Des Moines' go-to station for news, traffic, and severe weather updates. Your mornings kick off with The WHO Morning Show featuring Jeff Angelo at 4:59 a.m., delivering everything you need to start the day. At 9 a.m., tune in to Emery on Iowa for in-depth local discussions, followed by The Big Show from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m., America's premier farm radio show hosted by Bob Quinn. The afternoon continues with Clay Travis and Buck Sexton from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m., and wraps up with The Simon Conway Show from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m., bringing engaging talk and relevant news.
